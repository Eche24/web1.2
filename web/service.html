1. VAWA & Adjustment of Status (AOS)

For abused immigrants applying for lawful permanent residency under the Violence Against Women Act (VAWA). This service is for immigrant victims of abuse by their U.S. citizen or lawful permanent resident spouse.

Our team supports clients in preparing a VAWA self- petition (Form I-360) and an Adjustment of Status application (Form I-485) when eligible to apply from within the U.S., including assistance to secure a highly experienced psychologist or psychotherapist for a immigration assessment as required.
