About

At VAWA Help Center, we are a dedicated team of experienced immigration paralegals and immigration experts working under attorney supervision to help individuals and families navigate the U.S. immigration system—especially **VAWA-based green card and Adjustment of Status applications**.

Many of us have been in the same position as our clients: facing the overwhelming and often unaffordable challenge of preparing immigration paperwork, dealing with abusive circumstances, and not knowing where to turn. We understand what it means to start over in a new country, and we’re here to help others do the same—with **compassion, affordability, and excellence**.

Why We Exist

Every year, thousands of immigrants in the U.S. are forced to give up their hopes of legal status simply because they cannot afford high attorney fees. That’s why we created the VAWA Help Center—a nonprofit initiative made up of seasoned immigration professionals who have spent years working in the field, assisting abused immigrant victims to file for green cards through VAWA and other humanitarian pathways.

We specialize in **VAWA Self-Petitions, Marriage-Based Adjustment of Status, and U.S. Citizenship Applications**—particularly for those who are abused by their U.S. citizen or lawful permanent resident spouses, and are left without options or support.

What Makes Us Different?

  • Team of Experts: Our group consists of immigration paralegals and form preparation specialists, each with several years of experience, trained under highly rated immigration attorneys.
  • Attorney Supervision: All cases are managed and reviewed under licensed attorney oversight to ensure legal compliance and the highest service quality.
  • Affordability with Integrity: We believe that the American Dream should not come with a heavy price tag. That’s why we offer low-cost services with flexible payment plans—ensuring no one is left behind.
  • One-on-One Case Support: Every client receives personalized support throughout the immigration process. We keep up-to-date with the latest immigration laws and policies to better serve our clients and ensure successful results.

Our Results

With a **99.9% approval rate**, we’ve helped over **20+ immigrant victims of abuse** and numerous families complete their immigration applications successfully—without the stress of high fees or navigating the complex system alone.

What We Do as Immigration Form Specialists (IFS)

As Immigration Form Specialists operating under legal supervision, our team is authorized and equipped to provide the following services:

  • Objectively gather case facts and flag any legal red flags
  • Translate and interpret immigration instructions and forms
  • Assist in completing immigration forms with accuracy and care
  • Help collect required evidence and supporting documentation
  • Provide English translations for foreign documents
  • Organize and prepare full USCIS submission packages
  • Maintain detailed and confidential case files for every client
  • Issue written contracts, receipts, and retain full transparency
  • Immediately refer clients to attorneys for complex legal advice

Who We Serve

We assist undocumented immigrants, abuse survivors, low-income families, and anyone who has a straightforward case but needs professional help preparing their applications. With approximately **11 million undocumented immigrants in the U.S.**, and millions more waiting for their chance to adjust their status, our mission is clear: to **bridge the gap between affordability and excellent service**.
